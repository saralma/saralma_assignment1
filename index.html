My Film

Drawing on the unique dynamics of a hotline center, where each call introduces the caller to a critical aspect of the service provided, my film showcases a parallel to my own life. Acting as though my existence is centered around such a hotline, the narrative delves into a significant phase of my life attending NYUAD. This experience, akin to answering a life-altering call, has transformed me in countless ways. The film is aptly named 'Hello NYUAD' to symbolize the act of receiving a call that brings about monumental change, reflecting my journey and growth through my time at NYU Abu Dhabi
